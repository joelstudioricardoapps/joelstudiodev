Privacy Policy for Tap Dodger: Space Survival

This page explains how Joel Studio, C.A. handles information related to Tap Dodger: Space Survival and studio support communication.

Overview

Joel Studio, C.A. values user privacy. Tap Dodger: Space Survival is designed as a lightweight arcade game experience. This policy should always reflect the actual behavior of the released app and support channels.

Important: if the game later adds analytics, ads, cloud saving, account systems, payments, or third-party SDKs that collect data, this policy must be updated before release.

Information we collect

At the current stage, Tap Dodger: Space Survival is intended to operate without account registration and without requiring sensitive personal information from players during gameplay.

  • No sign-up or login is required to play.
  • Scores, achievements, and progress may be stored locally on the device for gameplay purposes.
  • If you contact Joel Studio, C.A. by email, we may receive the information you include in your message, such as your email address and support details.

How information is used

  • To provide gameplay features such as local progress, high scores, and unlocked content.
  • To respond to support questions and player inquiries.
  • To maintain and improve the game if issues are reported.

Data sharing

Joel Studio, C.A. does not sell user data. If the app is operating as described above, gameplay data remains local to the device unless a future feature clearly states otherwise.

Children's privacy

Tap Dodger is a simple arcade-style game. If the audience, content rating, or in-app functionality changes in the future, this policy and the Google Play declarations should be reviewed and updated accordingly.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to keep support communications and site content secure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

Joel Studio, C.A. may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any new version should be posted on this page with the latest effective date.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact: support@joelstudiodev.work.
